Music of the World

Learn about the songs and their origins!

🇧🇷Brazil: Bossa Nova

Song: Eu Vim da Bahia - João Gilberto

Bossa Nova, originating in Brazil in the late 1950s and early 1960s, is a smooth and laid-back style blending samba rhythms with jazz influences. Often characterized by its melodic guitar work and Portuguese lyrics, Bossa Nova, popularized by Antonio Carlos Jobim and João Gilberto, creates a relaxed and romantic musical atmosphere.

🇮🇳India: Bollywood

Song: Dilbar - from "Satyameva Jayate"

Bollywood music, emerging from the Indian film industry, is a vibrant and eclectic mix of various musical styles. Starting in the 1930s, Bollywood songs incorporate elements of classical Indian music, folk traditions, and Western influences. These catchy tunes, often accompanied by colorful dance sequences, play a significant role in India's popular culture.

🇪🇸Spain: Flamenco

Song: Mi Estrella Blanca - Fondo Flamenco

Flamenco, hailing from Spain and evolving over centuries, is a passionate and expressive art form that encompasses music, dance, and singing. With origins in the Andalusian region, flamenco features intricate guitar playing, emotional vocals, and rhythmic handclaps, embodying the rich cultural heritage of Spain.

🇺🇸USA: Funk

Song: I Love You More - George Duke

Funk, originating in the USA during the late 1960s and early 1970s, is a lively and dance-centric musical style. Spearheaded by iconic figures like James Brown, funk is characterized by its infectious rhythm, syncopated beats, and groovy basslines. With its emphasis on the downbeat and soulful vocals, funk has become a timeless genre that continues to inspire and influence various musical movements.

🇲🇳Mongolia: Mongolian Throat Singing

Song: Jargalant Altai - Batzorig Vaanchig

Mongolian throat singing, rooted in Mongolia's nomadic traditions, is a unique vocal technique where a singer produces multiple pitches simultaneously. Originating from the vast steppes of Central Asia, throat singing creates a mesmerizing and otherworldly sonic landscape, often reflecting the connection between nature and the nomadic way of life.

🇳🇬Nigeria: Afrobeats

Song: Calm Down - Rema

Afrobeats, originating in Nigeria in the early 21st century, is a dynamic and genre-blurring style influenced by traditional African rhythms, highlife, and Western pop music. With artists like Wizkid and Burna Boy leading the global charge, Afrobeats is characterized by infectious beats, Afro-fusion elements, and a celebration of African cultural diversity.
